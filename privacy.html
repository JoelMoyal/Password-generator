Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

pwasecurity ("we", "us", "our") operates the password generator at this website. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Password Generation (Manual Mode)

All password generation in Manual Mode happens entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API. No passwords, no inputs, and no settings are ever sent to any server. We have no servers that receive this data.

3. AI Mode

When you use AI Mode, the text description you type is sent to Cloudflare Workers AI (a third-party service operated by Cloudflare, Inc.) to generate a password suggestion. This is the only time any data leaves your device.

  • We do not log, store, or retain your AI prompts.
  • Cloudflare may process your request in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.
  • Do not include sensitive personal information in your AI description.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.
  • EU/EEA users are shown a consent prompt before advertising cookies are set.

5. Cookies

We use a single cookie/localStorage entry to remember your cookie consent preference. No other first-party cookies are set. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense as described above.

6. Analytics

We do not use any analytics services (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels).

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, there is nothing to access or delete on our end. For data held by Google, refer to Google's privacy controls.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via joelmoyal.com.
